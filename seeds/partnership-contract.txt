INFLUENCER PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT
=================================

This Influencer Partnership Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of October 1, 2024 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

COMPANY: Modern Living Co.
Address: 123 Market Street, San Francisco, CA 94103
Email: partnerships@modernliving.com

INFLUENCER: Jessica Chen (@lifestylewithlaura)
Address: 456 Oak Avenue, Los Angeles, CA 90028
Email: jessica@lifestylewithlaura.com
Platform: Instagram
Followers: 285,000 (as of September 25, 2024)

RECITALS
--------
WHEREAS, Company is engaged in the business of selling sustainable home goods and lifestyle products;
WHEREAS, Influencer maintains a social media presence focused on lifestyle, home decor, and sustainable living;
WHEREAS, Company desires to engage Influencer to create and publish content promoting Company's products;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

1. SCOPE OF WORK
----------------
Influencer agrees to create and publish the following content ("Deliverables"):

1.1 Instagram Content:
- Four (4) Instagram Feed Posts featuring Company products
- Eight (8) Instagram Stories featuring Company products
- Two (2) Instagram Reels (15-60 seconds each)
- One (1) Instagram Live session (minimum 20 minutes)

1.2 Content Requirements:
- All content must feature Company products authentically integrated into Influencer's lifestyle
- Content must align with Influencer's existing aesthetic and voice
- Content must include required disclosures per FTC guidelines
- Content must be original and not previously published

1.3 Posting Schedule:
- Week 1 (Oct 7-13): 1 Feed Post, 2 Stories
- Week 2 (Oct 14-20): 1 Feed Post, 2 Stories, 1 Reel
- Week 3 (Oct 21-27): 1 Feed Post, 2 Stories
- Week 4 (Oct 28-Nov 3): 1 Feed Post, 2 Stories, 1 Reel, 1 Live

2. COMPENSATION
---------------
2.1 Total Compensation: $15,000 USD

2.2 Payment Schedule:
- 50% ($7,500) upon execution of this Agreement
- 50% ($7,500) upon completion of all Deliverables

2.3 Product Compensation:
- Company will provide Influencer with $2,000 worth of products for content creation
- Products remain property of Influencer after campaign completion

2.4 Payment Method:
- Wire transfer or check within 30 days of invoice
- Influencer to provide W-9 form before first payment

3. CONTENT APPROVAL
-------------------
3.1 Influencer will submit content for Company approval 48 hours before scheduled posting
3.2 Company will approve or request revisions within 24 hours
3.3 Company may request one (1) round of reasonable revisions per piece of content
3.4 Influencer retains creative control over content style and voice

4. USAGE RIGHTS
---------------
4.1 License Grant:
Company receives a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to:
- Repost content on Company's social media channels
- Use content in Company's email marketing
- Feature content on Company's website
- Use content in digital advertising

4.2 License Duration: 12 months from posting date

4.3 Attribution:
Company must credit Influencer when reposting content: "@lifestylewithlaura"

4.4 Exclusions:
Company may NOT use content for:
- Print advertising without additional compensation
- Television commercials
- Billboards or outdoor advertising
- Third-party licensing

5. EXCLUSIVITY
--------------
5.1 During the campaign period (October 1 - November 3, 2024) and for 30 days thereafter, Influencer agrees not to:
- Promote competing home goods brands
- Create sponsored content for direct competitors

5.2 Competing brands include:
- [Competitor A]
- [Competitor B]
- [Competitor C]

5.3 Non-competing categories (allowed):
- Fashion and apparel
- Beauty and skincare
- Food and beverage
- Travel and hospitality

6. DISCLOSURE REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------
6.1 Influencer must include clear and conspicuous disclosure of partnership:
- Instagram Posts: "#ad" or "#sponsored" in first three lines of caption
- Instagram Stories: "Paid partnership with @modernlivingco" sticker
- Instagram Reels: "#ad" in caption
- Instagram Live: Verbal disclosure within first 30 seconds

6.2 Disclosures must comply with FTC Endorsement Guidelines

7. PERFORMANCE METRICS
----------------------
7.1 Expected Performance (not guaranteed):
- Average engagement rate: 4.5%+
- Estimated reach: 200,000+ accounts
- Estimated impressions: 500,000+

7.2 Reporting:
Influencer will provide Instagram Insights screenshots within 7 days of each post

8. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
----------------------------------
8.1 Influencer represents and warrants:
- Follower count is authentic (no purchased followers or bots)
- Engagement is organic (no purchased likes or comments)
- Content will be original and not infringe third-party rights
- Influencer has right to enter into this Agreement

8.2 Company represents and warrants:
- Products are safe and as described
- Company has right to enter into this Agreement
- Company will make timely payments

9. CONFIDENTIALITY
------------------
9.1 Confidential Information includes:
- Compensation terms
- Upcoming product launches
- Company's marketing strategies
- Campaign performance data

9.2 Influencer agrees not to disclose Confidential Information without Company's written consent

9.3 Exclusions: Information that is publicly available or independently developed

10. TERMINATION
---------------
10.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement:
- For material breach with 7 days written notice to cure
- Immediately if other party becomes insolvent

10.2 Upon termination:
- Company pays for completed Deliverables on pro-rata basis
- Influencer returns unused products
- Usage rights survive for already-posted content

11. INDEMNIFICATION
-------------------
11.1 Influencer indemnifies Company against claims arising from:
- Influencer's breach of this Agreement
- Infringement of third-party rights in content
- Violation of FTC guidelines

11.2 Company indemnifies Influencer against claims arising from:
- Product defects or safety issues
- Company's use of content beyond licensed scope

12. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
---------------------------
Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Total liability shall not exceed the total compensation paid under this Agreement.

13. GENERAL PROVISIONS
----------------------
13.1 Governing Law: State of California

13.2 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between parties

13.3 Amendments: Must be in writing and signed by both parties

13.4 Assignment: Neither party may assign without other party's written consent

13.5 Independent Contractor: Influencer is an independent contractor, not an employee

13.6 Force Majeure: Neither party liable for delays due to circumstances beyond reasonable control
