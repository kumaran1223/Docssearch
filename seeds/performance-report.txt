Q3 2024 Marketing Performance Report
=====================================

Reporting Period: July 1 - September 30, 2024
Prepared by: Analytics Team
Date: October 5, 2024

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
Q3 2024 showed strong performance across most channels, with digital advertising exceeding targets by 23% and social media engagement up 45% YoY. However, email marketing underperformed expectations, requiring strategic adjustments for Q4.

Overall Performance: 112% of target
Total Marketing Spend: $1.8M
Total Revenue Attributed: $8.1M
Overall ROAS: 4.5:1

KEY HIGHLIGHTS
--------------
✓ Exceeded revenue target by $900K
✓ Customer acquisition cost decreased 18%
✓ Social media followers grew by 42K
✓ Website traffic increased 34% YoY
✗ Email open rates declined 3.2%
✗ Paid search CPC increased 12%

CHANNEL PERFORMANCE
-------------------

1. PAID SEARCH ($450K spend)
Revenue: $2.1M
ROAS: 4.7:1
Clicks: 125,000
Conversions: 3,850
Conversion Rate: 3.08%
Average CPC: $3.60 (↑12% vs Q2)

Top Performing Keywords:
- "sustainable home decor" - $285K revenue
- "modern furniture sale" - $198K revenue
- "eco-friendly gifts" - $156K revenue

Insights: CPC inflation due to increased competition. Recommend expanding long-tail keyword strategy and improving Quality Scores.

2. SOCIAL MEDIA ADVERTISING ($380K spend)
Revenue: $1.9M
ROAS: 5.0:1
Impressions: 8.2M
Clicks: 164,000
Engagement Rate: 4.8%

Platform Breakdown:
- Instagram: $1.1M revenue (58% of total)
- Facebook: $580K revenue (31% of total)
- TikTok: $220K revenue (11% of total)

Best Performing Content:
- User-generated content: 6.2% engagement
- Behind-the-scenes videos: 5.8% engagement
- Product tutorials: 4.9% engagement

Insights: TikTok showing promising growth despite smaller budget allocation. Recommend increasing TikTok spend by 40% in Q4.

3. EMAIL MARKETING ($120K spend)
Revenue: $980K
ROAS: 8.2:1
Emails Sent: 2.4M
Open Rate: 24.5% (↓3.2% vs Q2)
Click Rate: 3.1%
Conversion Rate: 4.2%

Segment Performance:
- VIP Customers: 38% open rate, $420K revenue
- Recent Purchasers: 28% open rate, $310K revenue
- Inactive Subscribers: 12% open rate, $95K revenue

Insights: List fatigue evident in declining open rates. Recommend re-engagement campaign and list cleaning. Test new subject line strategies.

4. CONTENT MARKETING ($180K spend)
Revenue: $1.4M (attributed)
Blog Traffic: 285,000 visits
Video Views: 1.2M
Downloads (guides/ebooks): 8,500

Top Content:
- "10 Sustainable Living Tips" - 45K visits
- "Summer Home Refresh Guide" - 38K visits
- Product comparison videos - 320K views

Insights: Long-form content driving quality traffic with 4.2min average time on page. Video content showing strong engagement.

5. INFLUENCER PARTNERSHIPS ($220K spend)
Revenue: $1.1M
ROAS: 5.0:1
Influencers Activated: 15
Total Reach: 3.2M
Engagement: 156,000

Top Performers:
- @lifestylewithlaura (250K followers): $380K revenue
- @ecohomestyle (180K followers): $290K revenue
- @modernliving (150K followers): $210K revenue

Insights: Micro-influencers (10K-50K) showing better engagement rates (5.8%) vs macro-influencers (3.2%), but lower absolute revenue.

6. DISPLAY ADVERTISING ($280K spend)
Revenue: $840K
ROAS: 3.0:1
Impressions: 12.5M
Clicks: 87,500
CTR: 0.70%

Insights: Lowest ROAS of all channels. Recommend reducing budget by 30% and reallocating to higher-performing channels.

7. AFFILIATE MARKETING ($170K spend)
Revenue: $780K
ROAS: 4.6:1
Active Affiliates: 145
New Affiliates: 28

Top Affiliates:
- DealFinder.com: $245K revenue
- HomeDecorBlog.net: $180K revenue
- SustainableLiving.org: $155K revenue

Insights: Steady performance. Opportunity to recruit more affiliates in sustainability niche.

RECOMMENDATIONS FOR Q4
----------------------
1. Increase TikTok budget by 40% ($152K → $213K)
2. Reduce display advertising by 30% ($280K → $196K)
3. Implement email list cleaning and re-engagement campaign
4. Expand long-tail keyword strategy for paid search
5. Launch cart abandonment optimization program
6. Increase investment in user-generated content
7. Test new influencer tier: nano-influencers (1K-10K followers)
8. Develop retargeting campaign for cart abandoners
9. Create holiday gift guide content series
10. Implement A/B testing program for email subject lines

CONCLUSION
----------
Q3 2024 demonstrated strong overall performance with significant wins in social media and paid search. The strategic focus on sustainability messaging resonated well with our target audience. Moving into Q4, we'll optimize underperforming channels while scaling successful initiatives to capitalize on holiday shopping season.
